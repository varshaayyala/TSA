What are E-Bikes?

E-Bikes are regular bikes with added components that help cross obstacles like mountains and hills easier. It does not replace human power but it helps augment it. It helps you travel further without getting tired. The main component of the electric bike is the motor. The motor’s power can range from 200W to 1000W. The higher the power, the quicker it drains the battery.

Three Main Types of EBikes

Throttle Based

If you have a throttle based bike, you would push a button, or twist something to receive the electrical boost. You don’t need to pedal what-so-ever when in this mode.

Pedal Based

The pedal based one will have electrical assistance the entire time you ride your bike. It will operate like a normal bike with an extra boost. .

Hybrid Between

For the hybrid one you would press a button and then you would receive a pedal based push which will work just like the pedal based bike until you press the button again.
